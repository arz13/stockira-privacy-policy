Stockira Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Stockira ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Camera Permission Usage

Our mobile application "Stockira" requests camera permission for the following specific purposes:

1. QR Code Scanning

  • Purpose: To scan QR codes for store identification and attendance tracking
  • When Used: Only when you actively tap the QR scan button
  • Data Collected: QR code content (store identification data)
  • Storage: Results stored locally and on our servers for attendance tracking

2. Photo Capture

  • Purpose: To take photos during check-in and check-out processes for attendance verification
  • When Used: Only when you actively tap the camera button
  • Data Collected: Photos you choose to take
  • Storage: Photos stored securely on your device and our servers

Important Privacy Notes:

  • Camera is NEVER accessed in the background
  • Camera is NOT used for location tracking
  • You can revoke camera permission anytime in device settings
  • Photos are not shared with third parties
  • All data is encrypted and stored securely

Data Collection

We collect only the data necessary for attendance tracking:

  • Attendance timestamps
  • Store visit records
  • Photos you choose to take
  • Location data for attendance verification

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted during transmission
  • Secure servers with access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information

Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke camera permission anytime
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
